Our Story

Venkateshwara Beedi Puna is a traditional beedi manufacturing unit established in 2017 by Vemula Srinivas in Hanamkonda, Telangana. We specialize in handcrafting beedis using select tobacco and natural tendu leaves, supplying wholesale to local pan shops, kirana stores, and retailers across the Warangal division.

What We Do

  • 🍃 Hand-rolling beedis using traditional methods
  • 🌿 Sourcing quality raw tobacco and tendu leaves
  • 📦 Packaging in bundles of 25, 50, and 100 sticks
  • 🏪 Wholesale supply to local shop owners only
  • 🚚 Bulk orders with flexible quantities
